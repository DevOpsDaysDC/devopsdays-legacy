Hotels: Here is a list of some hotels in the neighborhood of the venue

  • The Kendall Hotel (http://www.kendallhotel.com/) boutique, really close to the venue
  • The Residence Inn Boston-Cambridge, also very close
  • Boston Marriott Cambridge

The venue is basically right at the MIT-Kendall subway stop, on the Red Line. Any hotel that is very close to MIT is very close to the venue, and any hotel on any of these subway stops will be easy to get to the venue from (in order of closeness):

  • Harvard Square, Red Line
  • Porter Square, Red Line
  • Davis Square, Red Line
