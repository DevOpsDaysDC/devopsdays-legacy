Conference Location

DevOpsDays Detroit 2016 is being held at the College for Creative Studies, A. Alfred Taubman Center for Design Education. The building address is: 460 W. Baltimore Ave, Detroit, MI 48202. Maps and Directions

The location is approximately 30 minutes via car from Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport (DTW)



Parking

The Taubman Center parking structure is located on Baltimore between Cass and 2nd Avenue.
